Privacy Policy


This is the Privacy Notice of empowerange.github.io (Website). This Notice provides information on the processing of personal data of the people who choose to contribute to the NGOs using our website. The Notice may be changed over time. Users of the website are advised to regularly review the Notice for possible changes.

WHY DO WE PROCESS YOUR PERSONAL DATA?

We need your personal data so that we can pass that on to the concerned NGO that you choose to contribute to, to establish the further communication between you and the NGO. We also might need your personal data to send you the updates about how your contributions are making a difference and for also sending you the newsletter. Also, if you’re opting to join our team, we may require your data for the application process, and for further communication.

HOW DO WE SECURE DATA?

Your personal data are treated as strictly confidential and Empowerange has taken appropriate security measures against loss or unlawful processing of this data.
