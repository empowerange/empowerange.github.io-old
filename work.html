How we work!

Providing the list of different NGOs to the citizens

Citizens can go through the collection of NGOs and can choose the NGO they wish to volunteer with

Connecting the citizens with the NGOs

After the citizen finalizes an NGO, Empowerange will connect the citizen with the concerned NGO. After that, either Empowerange or the concerned NGO will get in touch with the citizen with the further details on how to start contributing.

Contributing Phase

After the citizen gets contacted with all the details on various methods of contribution, the citizen can finally start making the contributions or volunteering with the concerned NGO(s).

Feedback

The contibutors, and the volunteers will be expected to share their feedback by sharing the experience, after they make their contributions to an NGO or after the end of the volunteering period. Feedbacks will help us to serve the society more efficiently.
